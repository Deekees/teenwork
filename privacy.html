Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how TeenWork collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Introduction

TeenWork ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share your personal information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, username, date of birth, and other information you provide during registration or profile updates.
  • Account Information: Login credentials, account preferences, and application history.
  • Job & Application Data: Details of jobs posted, applied for, or saved.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, and interactions with the platform.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the TeenWork platform.
  • To process registrations, job applications, and job postings.
  • To communicate with users regarding their accounts, applications, or support requests.
  • To improve our services, website functionality, and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms and policies.

4. Data Storage & Security

We store your data securely using industry-standard measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only. While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

TeenWork uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and deliver relevant content. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect site functionality.

6. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating the platform, subject to confidentiality agreements. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and users.

7. Children’s Privacy

TeenWork is designed for users aged 13–18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such data, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

  • You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • You may withdraw consent or object to certain data uses at any time.
  • To exercise your rights, contact us at info@teenwork.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

For questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy, please contact us at info@teenwork.com.
