Free Courses to Boost Your Employability

Stand out to employers and build your skills with these free online courses, handpicked for UK teens. All courses are accessible, beginner-friendly, and designed to help you succeed in your first job and beyond.

Why Do Employability Skills Matter?

Employability skills are the abilities and qualities that help you get, keep, and succeed in a job. They include things like communication, teamwork, problem-solving, and digital know-how. Building these skills now will help you feel more confident and prepared for your first job and for your future career!

Top Tips for Getting Hired


Be proactive: Don’t wait for jobs to come to you—ask around, check local shops, and use online job boards.
Tailor your CV: Make sure your CV matches the job you’re applying for. Highlight your skills and any experience, even if it’s volunteering or school projects.
Practice interviews: Ask a friend or family member to do a mock interview with you so you feel more confident.
Follow up: If you haven’t heard back after applying, it’s okay to politely check in with the employer.
Stay positive: Rejection happens to everyone. Keep learning and trying—you’ll get there!
