About TeenWork

TeenWork is a UK-based digital platform dedicated to connecting teenagers with safe, legal, and age-appropriate employment and skill-building opportunities.

Platform Overview

TeenWork is operated and maintained in the United Kingdom, providing a centralised resource for teenagers (ages 13–18), employers, and parents/guardians. The platform is designed to facilitate access to part-time, seasonal, and entry-level job opportunities that comply with UK employment law for young workers. All job postings are reviewed for suitability and legal compliance before publication.

How TeenWork Operates

1

Registration

Users create an account as a teen, employer, or parent/guardian. All users must provide accurate information and agree to the platform’s terms of use and privacy policy.

2

Job Posting & Search

Employers submit job listings, which are reviewed for compliance with UK child employment regulations. Teens can search and filter jobs by age, location, and sector.

3

Application & Tracking

Teens apply directly through the platform. Application status and history are accessible via a secure dashboard. Employers can manage applications and contact suitable candidates.

Key Features

Verified Listings

All job postings are screened for legal compliance and suitability for young people.

Compliance with UK Law

TeenWork only lists jobs that adhere to UK child employment regulations, including age, hours, and permitted work types.

Skill Development

Access to free online courses and resources to support employability and personal growth.

Secure User Dashboard

Personal dashboards for teens and employers to manage applications, job posts, and profiles securely.

Support & Guidance

Guidance for teens, parents, and employers on safe working practices and legal responsibilities.

Eligibility & Safeguarding

TeenWork is available to UK residents aged 13–18 who are seeking part-time or seasonal employment. Employers using the platform are required to comply with all relevant UK employment and safeguarding laws. All user data is managed in accordance with our privacy policy and GDPR requirements, ensuring a safe and secure environment for all participants.

Contact & Legal

For enquiries, support, or to report a concern, please contact info@teenwork.com. Information regarding data handling, user rights, and our legal obligations can be found on our Contact page and within our privacy policy. TeenWork is committed to transparency and compliance with all applicable UK laws and regulations.
