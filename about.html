About TeenWork

TeenWork is the UK's dedicated platform for helping young people (ages 13–18) find safe, local work opportunities and build real-world skills. We partner with employers, schools, and communities to empower teens with confidence, independence, and a head start on their future careers.

Our mission is to make the world of work accessible, safe, and rewarding for every teen—no matter their background or experience.

Why Choose TeenWork?

Verified job postings: Every job is checked to ensure it’s suitable and safe for young people.
Teen-friendly interface: Simple, clear, and designed for first-time job seekers.
Built for UK employment law: We only list jobs that comply with UK rules for young workers.
Supportive community: We offer tips, courses, and resources to help you succeed.
Free to use: No fees for teens or families—ever.

How TeenWork Works

1

Browse Jobs

Explore a wide range of summer and part-time jobs for teens across the UK, all in one place. Use filters to find jobs that match your age, location, and interests.

2

Apply Easily

Click “Apply Now” and fill out a simple form—no complicated CVs or cover letters needed. You’ll get a confirmation and can track your applications in your dashboard.

3

Build Skills & Experience

Start your first job, gain real-world skills, and earn your own money. Use our free courses and tips to stand out to employers and grow your confidence!
